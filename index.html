About Me

My name is Clifton Jadoo and I was originally born in FL then moved to NY. Currently a Recruiter for a series C tech startup called Compass where I work as an Internal Recruiter specializing in business development, product, engineering and operational recruitment.

I graduated summa cum laude with a B.S. in Psychology and a B.A in sociology from Brooklyn College and a M.S. in Project Management and Operations Management from NYU Tandon School of Engineering. From my graduate courses and professional technical recruitment experience, I grew curious regarding programming and have begun to taught myself how to code as well as am attending a General Assembly Front End Web Development class for my professional and personal development.
